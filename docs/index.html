Spoolman GO

Filament management for iPhone

Manage your 3D printing filament spools from your iPhone. Connects to your self-hosted Spoolman or FilaMan server — or explore the app in Demo Mode without any server.

  • Dashboard with inventory overview and low-stock warnings
  • Full CRUD for spools, filaments, vendors and locations
  • NFC read & write in OpenSpool 1.0 format
  • Supports Spoolman and FilaMan (Spoolman compatibility API)
  • Light, dark and system theme
  • No account, no cloud — your data stays with you
